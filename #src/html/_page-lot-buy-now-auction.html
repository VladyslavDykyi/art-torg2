Lot Description

Fernand Leger, French,1881-1955. This trio of ink drawings on paper is a stunning example of Fernand Leger's imagery during his peak period in the 1930's . Ink on paper. They are 38.5 cm H by 28 cm W, 39.5 cm H by 28 cm W , and 38.5 cm H by 28 cm W. They are signed F. L. 39. There is only wear on the edges of the paper as can be seen but no damage to the image. They have excellent provenance: Prince Darius Tayarken of London (stamp verso), Private UK Collection, Private American Collection at present. Fernand Léger was born in 1881, the same year both Picasso and Braque were born, in Normandy; his father was a substantial cattle grazer.
